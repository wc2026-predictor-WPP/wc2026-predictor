❓ How to Play

WPP P4 WC26 League  ·  Entry ₹500 per player
⚽ The basics

Pick the winner of each knockout match from the Round of 32 through to the Final. Get it right and you earn 3 points. Wrong or no pick = 0 points. Highest total points wins.

🔒 Pick deadline

Each match locks exactly 1 hour before kickoff. After that your pick is frozen — no changes. A live countdown on every match shows you how long you have left.

📊 Scoring
Correct winner pick+3 pts
Wrong pick / no pick0 pts

🏆 Prizes

Top 4 players share the prize pool: 50% (1st) · 30% (2nd) · 15% (3rd) · 5% (4th). Prize amounts shown live on the Leaderboard tab.

⚖ Tiebreaker

If players finish level on points, tiebreakers are applied in order: (1) correct WC winner prediction, (2) closest Final goals guess, (3) most picks made, (4) earliest registration. Your predictions are private — set them in My Profile.

📱 Tips
  • Check the orange nudge bar — it tells you which matches need a pick in the next 72 hrs
  • Set your WC winner + Final goals in My Profile — they only matter if you tie
  • Use All Picks tab to see what everyone picked once a match kicks off
  • Share the Leaderboard to your WhatsApp group to keep the banter going
ℹ Results are entered by the League admin after each match. Points update instantly for everyone.
